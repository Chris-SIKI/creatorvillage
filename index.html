The Community

Not a creator house.
A creator community.

Creator Village is 98 geodesic domes on a single gated property, 30 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip. 95 of those are creator homes — 9-meter Cleado domes with built-in solar and battery storage. The other 3 are 14-meter community centers with recording studios, podcast rooms, event spaces, and collaboration hubs.

Every creator lives here full-time. This isn't a 6-month lease or a pop-up. It's a permanent community where people build their lives together — filming together, guesting on each other's channels, co-hosting livestreams, cross-promoting daily. When one creator grows, everyone around them grows too.

How It Works

The creator journey.

Move in, connect to the community's tools, and start creating. Everything here is designed to help you grow.

1
Move In
Your own geodesic dome home — fully sustainable, solar-powered, designed for both living and creating.
2
Connect to Community Tools
Marketing analytics, audience growth tools, and the community platform. Built to help you understand your audience and grow smarter.
3
Start Creating
Your dome is your studio. Start producing from day one with the community, the backdrop, and brand partnerships to amplify everything you make.
4
Use Studios & Events
Recording studios, podcast rooms, event spaces. Creator Village hosts concerts, competitions, and socials that become content moments for everyone.
5
Collaborate & Grow
Work with community members on content, shows, and projects. Since studios are available to residents, you can invite people from outside the community to collaborate — bringing in fresh audiences for both sides.

How It's Funded

Brand partnerships.

Companies sponsor individual domes. Each creator's home becomes a branded content hub. Brands get access to authentic creators living in a community built for content — and that sponsorship revenue helps cover operating costs for the entire village.
Creators produce content, not ads. Product integrations and brand campaigns happen naturally because the community itself is the set. The more the community grows, the more costs are offset for residents. Surplus funds new Creator Village communities.
