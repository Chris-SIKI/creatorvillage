The Opportunity

Your product becomes the set.

Creator Village is a gated community of 100 Cleado geodesic domes in Clark County, Nevada — 30 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip. 95 content creators live here full-time, producing daily content from inside their Cleado homes. Every video, every livestream, every photo has a Cleado dome as the literal backdrop.

Nobody scrolls past a dome. Traditional housing is invisible on social media. Nobody films their apartment hallway. Nobody shares their subdivision. But 100 geodesic domes in the Nevada desert? That's something people stop and say “what is that?” — and that curiosity is worth more than any ad buy. Every piece of content created here features Cleado’s product front and center, without it ever feeling like a commercial.
This isn’t a sponsorship. It’s infrastructure. Sponsorships end. Campaigns expire. Creator Village is a permanent, physical community where 95 creators wake up inside Cleado domes every single day. When one creator films a dome tour that gets 2M views, that’s Cleado marketing. When a brand shoots a campaign on-site, the dome is the backdrop. When press covers the community, it’s “the Cleado dome village.” The architecture markets itself.
The Core Thesis
Instead of running ads, Cleado gets 95 content creators living inside the product and filming it every day. At even modest audience sizes (10K–100K per creator), the combined monthly impressions across 95 creators reach tens of millions — all featuring Cleado as the literal backdrop. That’s organic product placement at a scale no campaign can match.
How It Works

The content flywheel.

Every stage of the Creator Village lifecycle generates marketing value for Cleado — from construction to daily life to scale.

1
Domes Go Up
100 Cleado domes deployed on 7.7 acres. Time-lapse video of flat-packed panels becoming livable homes in 12 hours. The deployment story alone is a viral moment — millions of views before a single creator moves in.
2
Creators Move In
95 content creators move into their Cleado dome homes. Day-one content: move-in vlogs, dome tours, “I live in a dome” reveals. Each one is authentic, shareable, and features the product in real lived context.
3
Daily Content
Every YouTube vlog, TikTok, Instagram story, and livestream is filmed inside or in front of a Cleado dome. The product is visible in every frame — not as an ad, but as someone’s actual home.
4
Brands Move In
Fortune 500 sponsors operate campaigns inside Cleado domes. Brand adjacency elevates Cleado from “dome manufacturer” to “the infrastructure behind major brand activations.”
5
Press & Media
A dome village for creators in the Nevada desert is inherently a story. Architecture, tech, housing, creator economy press all cover the community. Cleado is in every headline without a single pitch.
6
Scale
Revenue funds new Creator Village sites. Each new village = another 100+ dome purchase order for Cleado, and another permanent marketing engine in a new market.
Compounding Returns
Creators grow their audiences → more eyes on Cleado domes → more brand sponsors want in → more revenue for the village → ability to fund new Creator Village sites → more Cleado dome orders. Each new village is another 100-dome purchase and another permanent marketing engine.

Market Timing

Why now.

Three forces are converging that make Creator Village — and its marketing value for Cleado — uniquely powerful right now.

Creator Economy Explosion
$250B+ in 2024, projected to hit $480B+ by 2027. Brands are moving budgets from traditional advertising to creator partnerships. A community of 95 creators is an irresistible content distribution network — and every piece of content features Cleado.
U.S. Housing Crisis
50%+ of renters are cost-burdened. Traditional construction: 6–12 months, $300K+ per home. Cleado: 12 hours, $57K. The affordability gap creates massive demand for alternative housing — and Creator Village puts Cleado’s solution in front of millions of viewers.
Solar + Sustainability Wave
Consumer demand for sustainable housing is at an all-time high. Cleado’s integrated solar makes every dome a zero-energy-bill story. Creator Village puts that narrative in front of audiences daily — not as a whitepaper, but as lived experience.
