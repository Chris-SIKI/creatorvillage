Execution Plan

Four phases. 18 months.

Each phase has clear milestones, budgets, and go/no-go criteria. Capital is deployed only as milestones are met — protecting investor capital at every stage.

1
Months 1 – 6

Pre-Development & Regulatory

Phase budget: ~$5.4M (including $5M land acquisition)

⚖
Legal & Counsel
501(c)(3) nonprofit already established via RitePlan. Engage real-estate counsel for land purchase closing, title review, and zoning representation before Clark County Planning Commission.
$15K
⚑
Land Acquisition
Secure 7.7-acre parcel in Clark County. Pursue BLM SNPLMA nomination for government pricing (~$5M savings potential). Complete Phase I ESA, ALTA survey, and title.
$5,000,000
⚙
PE Stamp & Structural Engineering
Licensed Nevada PE reviews and stamps Cleado’s existing structural drawings for 2 dome designs (9m residential, 14m community). Site-specific calcs for Clark County wind/seismic loads and foundation connection details. Two design packages, not 100 individual stamps.
$10K
⚓
Clark County Zoning & PUD
PUD (Planned Unit Development) application for geodesic dome community. Includes planning consultant, filing fees (50% affordable housing reduction eligible), traffic impact study, neighborhood meeting, and Planning Commission hearing. File Alternate Materials & Methods (Form 1003) for LECA foundations.
$75K
★
Cleado Regulatory Package
Complete remaining 2 items for Clark County: (1) ASTM E119 or equivalent fire rating certification, (2) LECA foundation engineering validation. Compile full documentation package: wind load, seismic, thermal, energy compliance.
$50K
♦
Grant & Bond Applications
Bond counsel for tax-exempt bond issuance (~$50K), LIHTC application to Nevada Housing Division ($5K fee), and grant writer for HOME/CDBG/AB 540 Housing Trust Fund applications. Bond counsel fees fall under the IRS 2% cost-of-issuance cap.
$85K
✎
Civil Engineering & Site Design
Civil site plan for 7.7 acres: 100 dome pad layout, grading & drainage plan, utility plans (water, sewer, storm drain, electrical), street improvement plans, SWPPP, and landscape plan. Dome design is Cleado’s — this covers site/civil only (~$1,650/lot).
$165K
2
Months 6 – 10

Site Work & Infrastructure

Phase budget: ~$3.8M

⚒
Grading & Site Prep
Mass grading of 7.7 acres, soil compaction, erosion control, and drainage grading. Desert terrain requires minimal cut/fill — estimated 4–6 weeks.
$450K
⋯
Roads & Pathways
Bio-enzyme + chip seal road surfacing (saves $550K–$1.4M vs traditional asphalt). E-W primary roads at 12m width, N-S secondary roads at 7m. Pedestrian pathways between dome clusters.
$425K
☷
Water & Wastewater
LVVWD water main connection, internal distribution, fire hydrants, and domestic service lines to each pad. Wastewater: gravity sewer mains, laterals, and connection to Clark County WRD.
$1,175,000
⚡
Electrical & Internet
NV Energy transformer and underground primary distribution. 200A service to each dome pad. Fiber-to-the-dome internet via Cox or municipal provider. Solar-ready conduit stubs.
$515K
♿
Foundations (100 Pads)
Pour 100 reinforced circular foundation pads (LECA or conventional, pending Form 1003 approval). Includes anchor bolt patterns matched to Cleado dome base rings.
$875K
☣
Fire Suppression
Fire main loop, hydrant placement per Clark County Fire code spacing, standpipe connections for community domes. Coordinate with CCFD for plan review and acceptance test.
$325K
3
Months 10 – 14

Dome Deployment & Finishing

Phase budget: ~$7.2M

⛺
Residential Dome Install (97 units)
Cleado 9m geodesic domes shipped and assembled on-site. Each dome assembles in ~12 hours. Phased deployment: 25 domes/month across 4 months. Factory-built panels arrive flat-packed.
$5,529,000
⛪
Community Domes (3 units)
Three 14m Cleado domes for shared spaces: content studio, co-working lounge, and community kitchen/event space. Higher structural load for AV equipment, HVAC, and commercial kitchen.
$540K
◔
Perimeter Wall & Gate
CMU block or desert-aesthetic perimeter wall with controlled entry gate, keycard/app access, visitor intercom, and security camera integration.
$250K
♨
Community Pool & Amenity Area
Resort-style pool, lounge deck, shade structures, and outdoor content filming area. Pool equipment sized for 100-resident community. Clark County Health District permitting.
$350K
⏻
EV Charging Stations
Level 2 chargers at community parking area (10–15 stalls). NV Energy commercial EV rate. NEVI or NV Energy rebate offsets estimated at 30–50% of install cost.
$200K
☘
Desert Landscaping
Xeriscape design: native Nevada plants, decomposed granite pathways, drip irrigation, shade trees at community areas. SNWA water-smart certification for reduced water rates.
$375K
4
Months 14 – 18

Creator Move-In & Revenue Launch

Phase budget: Operations & marketing

✍
Creator Selection & Onboarding
Open applications for 95 creator residents. Selection criteria: content quality, audience size, collaboration potential, and community fit. Lease signing and move-in scheduling.
✈
Brand Partnership Activation
Activate naming rights, dome sponsorships, and branded content deals. Target: 5–10 anchor brand partners pre-launch. Revenue from brand partnerships helps offset resident operating costs.
⚙
Content Studio Setup
Outfit community domes with production equipment: lighting rigs, soundproofing, green screens, podcast booths, streaming setups. High-speed fiber backbone activated.
🏁
Grand Opening & Media Launch
Phased move-in over 4 weeks. Launch event with resident creators producing day-one content. Press coverage, social media blitz, and brand activation events.
♻
Ongoing Operations
Property management, community programming, creator support services, brand relationship management, and maintenance. Estimated annual operating cost: ~$700K covered by sponsorship and partnership revenue.

Cleado Regulatory Readiness
Cleado's dome system is nearly ready for Clark County approval. Only 2 items remain: (1) ASTM E119 fire rating certification, and (2) LECA foundation engineering validation. Wind load (115 mph / Exposure C), seismic (SDC D), thermal (IECC CZ 3B), and energy compliance documentation are already complete. Estimated timeline to full compliance: 3–4 months from engagement.
Capital Protection
Capital is deployed in phases — not all at once. Each phase has clear go/no-go criteria before the next tranche is released. Phase 1 (pre-development) secures regulatory approval and land before any construction capital is committed. If zoning or permits fail, exposure is limited to ~$1.8M in soft costs.
